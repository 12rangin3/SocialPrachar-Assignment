Preparation Time

  • Total Approximetly 30 minute
  • Preparation Approximetly 7 minute
  • Cooking 30 minute

ingredients

  • 2-3 large eggs
  • salt to taste
  • Proper to taste
  • butter and oil
  • Optional- vegetable meat-masala etc....

Instructions

  1. Crack the eggs into a bowl, add salt, and whisk until well combined.
  2. Heat a non-stick skillet over medium heat and add butter or oil.
  3. Pour the egg mixture into the skillet and cook until the edges start to set.
  4. Gently lift the edges with a spatula, allowing uncooked egg to flow underneath.
  5. Add any optional ingredients like cheese or vegetables, fold the omelette in half, and cook for another minute.
  6. Slide the omelette onto a plate and serve hot.
